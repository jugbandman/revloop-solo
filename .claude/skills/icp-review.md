# ICP Creation and Review

Build or refine the Ideal Customer Profile. Define target segments, buyer personas, and qualification criteria.

## Prerequisites

- Read `60-Context/icp.md` if it exists (for review/refinement)
- Read `60-Context/offerings.md` for what we sell and who it helps

## Workflow

### 1. Understand Current State

Ask the user:
- What do you sell? (Product/service, if not already in 60-Context/offerings.md)
- Who have you sold to before? (Check 80-Pipeline/closed/ for existing customer data)
- What market hypotheses do you have?
- What segments are you considering?

If `60-Context/icp.md` exists, read it and present the current ICP for review.

### 2. Identify Target Segment

A strong segment is:
- Specific enough to target effectively
- Large enough to build a business on
- Accessible (you can reach them)
- Has urgent pain you solve

Define:
- Company size (employees, revenue, stage)
- Industry/industries
- Geography
- Other characteristics (tech stack, hiring patterns, recent funding)

### 3. Define Buyer Personas

For each persona:
- **Title/Role**: What do they call themselves?
- **Department**: Where do they sit?
- **Goals**: What are they measured on?
- **Pain Points**: What keeps them up at night?
- **Decision Authority**: Budget holder, influencer, or champion?
- **Where they hang out**: LinkedIn groups, conferences, publications

### 4. Establish Qualification Criteria

Three dimensions:

**Fit (Do they match ICP?):**
- Company characteristics checklist
- Role/title alignment
- Industry match

**Urgency (Do they have a pressing need?):**
- "Why now" triggers (initiatives, hiring, competitive pressure)
- Signals to watch for

**Ability to Act (Can they decide and purchase?):**
- Decision maker engagement indicators
- Budget signals
- Timeline reasonableness

### 5. Document the ICP

Save the complete ICP to `60-Context/icp.md` with:
- Target segment definition
- Buyer personas
- Qualification criteria (fit, urgency, ability to act)
- Key pain points
- "Why now" triggers
- Disqualification criteria (who is NOT a fit)

### 6. Validate (Optional)

If the user wants to validate:
- Research sample companies matching the segment
- Check if buyer personas exist at those companies
- Present findings and refine criteria

## Voice Rules

- ICP documentation is internal, direct language is fine
- Keep it concise and actionable, not academic
